# OrbitalViewer Tutorial Script (~2 min)

Hi everyone, today I'm going to introduce OrbitalViewer. First, under the "Path Settings" tab, click Browse to configure the paths for Multiwfn.exe and VMD — Tachyon will be auto-detected from the VMD directory. Then in the "Input File" section, choose Single File mode, browse to load your .fchk file, and specify an output directory where the generated cube files and rendered images will be saved. Switch to the "Orbital & Render" tab. In the orbital selection area, you can use the shortcut buttons to auto-fill HOMO+LUMO, or manually enter orbities like h-1,h,l,l+1 separated by commas. The default isovalue is 0.05 and medium grid works fine. For rendering parameters, there are 11 built-in styles from vcube2.0 — pick one you like, set resolution to 2000×1500, full coloring, and check trans_raster3d for faster rendering. Next, go to the "Preview & Run" tab, click "Generate Cube", and Multiwfn runs in the background — it only takes a few seconds. Then click "Preview (Single)" and the VMD window pops up showing the orbital isosurface and molecular structure. Drag with the mouse to rotate, scroll to zoom, and find the best viewing angle. Once satisfied, go back and click "Render Current View" — Tachyon ray-tracing outputs a high-res image directly. In the LIVE ADJUSTMENTS section, drag the isovalue slider from 0.005 to 0.5 to resize the orbital surface in real time inside VMD — super smooth. The opacity slider works the same way: drop it to 0.3 to see the skeleton through the surface. Finally, switch to the "Tools" tab: click "Hide All Hydrogens" to clean up the clutter and highlight the heavy-atom framework. In the "Dashed Bonds" tool, enter two atom indices, choose dashed style, gray color, semi-transparent material, and click Draw — a nice dashed line appears in VMD, perfect for indicating hydrogen bonds or interactions. That's the complete workflow from fchk to a polished orbital image in OrbitalViewer. Thanks for watching!

大家好，今天介绍 OrbitalViewer。首先在「路径设置」标签页，点击浏览分别配置 Multiwfn.exe 和 VMD 的路径，Tachyon 会自动从 VMD 目录读取。然后在「输入文件」区域选择单个文件模式，浏览载入你的 .fchk 文件，再指定一个输出目录，生成的 cube 和图片都会存到这里。切换到「轨道与渲染」标签页，在轨道选择中可以用快捷按钮一键填入 HOMO+LUMO，也可以手动输入如 h-1,h,l,l+1，逗号分隔即可，等值面默认 0.05，网格选中等就行。渲染参数这里内置了 11 种 vcube2.0 风格，选一个喜欢的，分辨率选 2000x1500，着色完整，勾选 trans_raster3d 加速。接下来切到「预览运行」标签页，点击「生成 Cube」，后台自动调用 Multiwfn 计算，几秒钟就好了。然后点「预览单个」，VMD 窗口弹出，显示轨道等值面和分子结构，鼠标拖拽旋转视角、滚轮缩放，找到最佳拍摄角度。调好后回到主界面点「渲染当前视图」，Tachyon 光线追踪直接输出高清图片。在 LIVE ADJUSTMENTS 区域，拖动等值面滑块可以在 0.005 到 0.5 之间实时调整轨道面大小，VMD 中同步变化非常丝滑；透明度滑块也一样，调到 0.3 能看到骨架穿透效果。最后切到「工具」标签页，点击「隐藏所有氢原子」滤掉杂乱的 H，突出重原子结构。在「绘制虚线键」中输入两个原子编号，类型选虚线，颜色灰色，半透明材质，点绘制就在 VMD 中出现了漂亮的标注线，适合指示氢键或相互作用。以上就是 OrbitalViewer 从 fchk 到精美轨道图的全流程。感谢观看！
